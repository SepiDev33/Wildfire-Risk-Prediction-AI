# Project Proposal

## Title
Wildfire Risk Prediction Using Machine Learning

## Problem
Wildfires are becoming more frequent and destructive due to climate
change and extreme environmental conditions.

Predicting wildfire risk can help emergency responders and
communities prepare in advance.

## Approach
This project will explore the use of machine learning techniques to
predict wildfire risk based on environmental data such as temperature,
humidity, wind speed, and rainfall.

## Dataset
Public wildfire datasets from sources such as NASA and Kaggle will
be used to train the model.

## Expected Outcome
The goal is to develop a model that can estimate wildfire risk levels
based on environmental conditions.
